Firefox for Android

This project is sponsored by Mozilla and UCOSP. I am working with a team of university students to develop features that will create a better user experience in Firefox for Android. My team has been tasked with the mission of developing two features:

1. Allow Firefox for Android to support right to left language specific layouts (Bug: 702845)

2. Develop a feature that allows users to switch the layout to support left handed usability (Bug: 702959)


My blog about developing for Firefox.
